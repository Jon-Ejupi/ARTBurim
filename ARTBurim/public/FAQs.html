Gallery Information

Why we are online gallery.

Why We're Online

Our digital gallery brings art directly to you, anytime, anywhere. This global reach means more visibility for artists and a wider, more diverse selection for collectors. By operating online, we cut down on traditional overheads, allowing us to offer fairer terms for artists and great value for buyers. Our focus remains squarely on showcasing stunning artwork in its best light, connecting artists with a worldwide audience without geographical limits.

Art Purchasing & Shipping

How do I purchase artwork from your gallery?

You can purchase artwork through several methods:

  1. Online: Our website features available works with secure checkout.
  2. Phone: Call us at +49 177 383 953 7 to discuss available pieces.
  3. Email: Contact us at burimajdini.ba@gmail.com for purchase inquiries.

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and payment plans can be arranged for significant purchases.

Exaple: PayPal, Visa, Gpay.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship artwork worldwide. All shipments are handled by professional fine art shippers with expertise in handling valuable artwork. Shipping costs vary based on:

  • Size and weight of the artwork
  • Destination country
  • Shipping method (standard, expedited, or white-glove service)
  • Insurance requirements

We provide a detailed shipping quote with every purchase. Most international shipments take 7-14 business days, excluding customs processing.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our return policy includes:

  • 14-day return period: You may return artwork within 14 days of receipt for a full refund (less shipping costs).
  • Condition requirements: Artwork must be returned in its original condition and packaging.
  • Return shipping: Buyer is responsible for return shipping costs and insurance.
  • Commissioned works: Custom or commissioned pieces are non-refundable.

Please contact us before returning any artwork to initiate the return process.

Can I commission a custom piece?

Yes, we frequently arrange custom commissions with our artists. The process typically involves:

  1. Initial consultation to discuss your vision, size requirements, and budget
  2. Artist selection based on style compatibility
  3. Preliminary sketches and concept development
  4. Approval of final concept and contract signing
  5. Creation of the artwork (timeline varies by artist and complexity)
  6. Delivery and installation

Commission fees generally start at €3,000 and include the artist's fee and our advisory services. A 50% deposit is required to begin the process.

Do you offer art consultation services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive art consultation services for both private collectors and corporate clients. Our services include:

  • Collection development and strategy
  • Artwork selection for specific spaces and interiors
  • Commission management
  • Art investment advice
  • Collection management and documentation
  • Installation coordination

Consultation fees vary based on project scope. Initial 30-minute consultations are complimentary. Contact us to schedule an appointment with one of our art advisors.
